Descripción


            

              Resolvemos la ecuación de calor 1D:
            


            

              \[
              \partial_t u = k\,\partial_{xx}u,\quad x\in[0,10],\ t\in[0,10].
              \]
            


            

              Con condiciones de frontera \(u(t,0)=u_0\) y \(u(t,10)=u_{10}\),
              el estado estacionario es:
            


            

              \[
              u_s(x) = u_0 + \frac{u_{10}-u_0}{10}\,x,
              \]
            


            

              mostrado como línea punteada. Puedes activar los
              primeros 5 modos (gradiente verde) y mover el deslizador de
              tiempo.
            


            
Solución explícita (modos de Fourier) — condición inicial uniforme


            

            Para \(u_t=k\,u_{xx}\) en \(x\in[0,L]\) con \(u(0,t)=u_0\), \(u(L,t)=u_L\) y
            condición inicial uniforme \(u(x,0)=C\), escribimos
            \(u(x,t)=u_s(x)+v(x,t)\), donde
            \[
            u_s(x)=u_0+\frac{u_L-u_0}{L}\,x
            \]
            es el estado estacionario y \(v\) satisface fronteras homogéneas.
            Entonces
            \[
            v(x,t)=\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} b_n\,e^{-k\left(\frac{n\pi}{L}\right)^2 t}\,
            \sin\!\left(\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right),
            \]
            con coeficientes
            \[
            b_n=\frac{2}{L}\int_{0}^{L}\big(C-u_s(x)\big)\,
            \sin\!\left(\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right)\,dx.
            \]
            En particular, al evaluar la integral se obtiene
            \[
            b_n=\frac{2}{n\pi}\Big[(C-u_0)\big(1-(-1)^n\big)+(u_L-u_0)(-1)^n\Big].
            \]
            (En este mathlet usamos \(L=10\).)

Description


            

              We solve the 1D heat equation:
            


            

              \[
              \partial_t u = k\,\partial_{xx}u,\quad x\in[0,10],\ t\in[0,10].
              \]
            


            

              With boundary values \(u(t,0)=u_0\) and \(u(t,10)=u_{10}\), the
              steady state is:
            


            

              \[
              u_s(x) = u_0 + \frac{u_{10}-u_0}{10}\,x,
              \]
            


            

              shown as a dashed line. You can show the
              first 5 modes (green gradient) and move the
              time slider.
            


            
Explicit solution (Fourier modes) — uniform initial condition


            

            For \(u_t=k\,u_{xx}\) on \(x\in[0,L]\) with \(u(0,t)=u_0\), \(u(L,t)=u_L\), and
            uniform initial condition \(u(x,0)=C\), write
            \(u(x,t)=u_s(x)+v(x,t)\), where the steady state is
            \[
            u_s(x)=u_0+\frac{u_L-u_0}{L}\,x
            \]
            and \(v\) satisfies homogeneous Dirichlet boundaries. Then
            \[
            v(x,t)=\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} b_n\,e^{-k\left(\frac{n\pi}{L}\right)^2 t}\,
            \sin\!\left(\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right),
            \]
            with coefficients
            \[
            b_n=\frac{2}{L}\int_{0}^{L}\big(C-u_s(x)\big)\,
            \sin\!\left(\frac{n\pi x}{L}\right)\,dx.
            \]
            Evaluating the integral gives
            \[
            b_n=\frac{2}{n\pi}\Big[(C-u_0)\big(1-(-1)^n\big)+(u_L-u_0)(-1)^n\Big].
            \]
            (In this mathlet we use \(L=10\).)
            


            
Mathlet features

      •

      •

                \]
                shown as a dashed line.
