3D linear system


            

              This applet plots solutions of:
            


            

              \[
              \left\{
              \begin{aligned}
              \dot{x} &= a_{11}x+a_{12}y+a_{13}z,\\
              \dot{y} &= a_{21}x+a_{22}y+a_{23}z,\\
              \dot{z} &= a_{31}x+a_{32}y+a_{33}z.
              \end{aligned}
              \right.
              \]
            


            

              In matrix form: \(\dot{X}=AX\). The origin is always a critical point, and its
              qualitative behavior is determined by the eigenvalues of \(A\).
            


            


            
How to use

            
    
              
  • Adjust the 9 parameters \(a_{ij}\).
    • 
              
  • Choose \((x_0,y_0,z_0)\) and click Add trajectory.
    • 
              
  • Overlay multiple solutions to compare behavior.
    • 
              
  • Use ✖ to remove trajectories.
