Javier Peraza

PhD
Perimeter Institute, Waterloo, Canada

I am a researcher and educator with training in both physics and mathematics, having completed two PhD programs in these areas. My research interests include numerical methods, general relativity, field theory, probability, and geometric analysis, and I often work across domains where these tools intersect. Alongside research, I have experience as a teaching assistant and adjunct professor. I am also an active advocate for outreach and have worked for many years with students of different ages, helping them engage with mathematics, physics, and science in general. I welcome collaboration in multidisciplinary settings and contribute a versatile skill set in coding, mathematics, physics, and analytical research.
